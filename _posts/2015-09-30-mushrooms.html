We found a mushroom and egg recipe that looked pretty neat, so we gave it a shot with our own little spin on it.

We both really liked cooking up these mushrooms. It was pretty easy and didn't take a lot of time, and it tasted great too.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon shallot minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup favorite arrabiata sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese grated
  • parsley to garnish

Turn broiler on high heat. Wipe mushrooms clean with a paper towel, and place on foil lined baking sheet. Place in the broiler for 5 minutes, then flip over for 5 more minutes. Turn the oven to bake at 400F. Spoon a bit of the arrabiata sauce into each mushroom, and sprinkle with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Crack an egg into each mushroom, and sprinkle with shallots and garlic. Put back in oven for 15 minutes or until eggs whites are completely cooked and firm. Top with Parmesan cheese and parsley.
