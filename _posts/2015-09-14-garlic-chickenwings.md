Ingredients: 
18 frozen chicken wings
3 cups Canola or Vegetable oil
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1/2 cup unsalted butter
2 ounces hot sauce (Tabasco or Tapatio)
